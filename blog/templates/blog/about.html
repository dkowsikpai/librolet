About

Librolet is a non profit initiative by syscodefy that aims at faciliating sharing of books.By mediating the renter and the rentee,Librolet adds new dimensions to the conventional renting process through cashless exchange of books.It also provides communication facilities which enables agreements and contracts between the renter and rentee.With the youth drifting away from books to mobile gadget,Librolet's primary objective includes instilling a sense of readership among students.It also forms the perfect platform for the elderly who are already into the habit of reading.
